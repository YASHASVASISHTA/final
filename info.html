JEE Main Exam Pattern (2026)

Purpose Admission to NITs, IIITs, CFTIs, and qualifying for JEE Advanced.
Exam Mode Computer-based Test (CBT), except for the Drawing section in B.Arch.
Duration 3 hours (180 minutes) per paper.
Marking Scheme +4 for correct answer, -1 for incorrect answer (for both MCQs and Numerical Value Questions).
Subjects & Structure
  • **Paper 1 (B.E./B.Tech):** 75 questions total (25 each from Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics). Each subject has 20 MCQs (Section A) and 10 Numerical Value Questions (Section B), where 5 must be attempted. Total 300 Marks.
  • **Paper 2A (B.Arch):** Mathematics, Aptitude Test, and Drawing.

NEET (UG) Exam Pattern (2026)

Purpose Admission to MBBS, BDS, and AYUSH (BAMS, BHMS, etc.) courses across India.
Exam Mode Offline (Pen-and-paper OMR-based).
Duration 3 hours and 20 minutes (200 minutes).
Marking Scheme +4 for correct answer, -1 for incorrect answer. Total 720 Marks.
Subjects & Structure
  • 180 Multiple-Choice Questions (MCQs) to be attempted out of 200 questions (50 questions in each subject section, divided into A and B).
  • Physics (45 Qs), Chemistry (45 Qs), Biology (Botany & Zoology - 90 Qs).

KCET Exam Pattern (2026)

Purpose Admission to Engineering, Pharmacy, Agriculture, and other UG courses in Karnataka.
Exam Mode Offline (Pen-and-paper OMR-based).
Duration 80 minutes for each subject paper (Physics, Chemistry, Maths/Biology).
Marking Scheme +1 for correct answer. **NO NEGATIVE MARKING.**
Subjects & Structure
  • 60 questions in each subject (Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics/Biology).
  • Total 180 questions (excluding Kannada Language Test).
